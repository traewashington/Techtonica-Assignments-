Walnut Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Soft and Chewy Walnut Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

*Simple, Sweet and Always a Favorite*

These Walnut Chocolate Chip Cookies are the perfect balance of chewy, nutty, and sweet.Each bite combines gooey melted chocolate with the rich crunch of toasted walnuts, creating a bakery-style cookie that feels homemade in the best way. This simple, classic recipe comes together in under 30 minutes and is one anyone can master. No special equipment or fancy tricks required. Whether you’re baking for a quiet night in or sharing a batch with loved ones, these cookies bring that “fresh from the oven” warmth that never fails to comfort.

Ingredients

  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 cup (2 sticks) unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 ½ cups semisweet chocolate chips (or chunks)
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts (lightly toasted for extra flavor)

Recipe Details

  • Makes: 24 cookies
  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Bake Time: 10-12 minutes
  • Total Time: 25-30 minutes

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Line two baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until light and fluffy (about 2–3 minutes).
  4. In the wet ingredients bowl, mix in eggs one at a time, then stir in vanilla extract.
  5. Gradually add the flour mixture to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.
  6. Fold in chocolate and walnuts: Stir in chocolate chips and chopped walnuts evenly
  7. Use a spoon or a cookie scoop to make rounded circles of dough.
  8. Place dough onto the prepared baking sheets, spacing them about 2 inches apart.
  9. Bake for 10–12 minutes, or until edges are golden and centers still look slightly soft.
  10. Let cookies rest on the baking sheet for 2–3 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
  11. Enjoy your cookies!

Optional Add-Ins

  • Sprinkle a little sea salt on top before baking for a sweet-salted touch.
  • Swap half the walnuts for pecans or macadamia nuts for variety.
  • Use dark chocolate chunks for a richer, deeper flavor.
